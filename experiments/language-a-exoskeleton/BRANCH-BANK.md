# Deterministic branch bank

After stop, quarantine, cancellation, integrity, census, and analysis-admissibility gates, evaluate every predicate and bank the first satisfied predicate in this order:

1. B-HARM
2. B-INTERACTION
3. B-NOTATION
4. B-SCAFFOLD
5. B-NULL
6. B-INCONCLUSIVE

B-HARM: a core contrast worsens burden by at least 0.10 with its 95 percent interval wholly beyond the margin, or any frozen completeness, refusal/abstention, utility, cost, truncation, over-bounding, deletion, or omission harm gate fires. Name the harmed contrast and stratum/surface. Integrity failure is not substantive harm.

B-INTERACTION: the predeclared family D_N range exceeds 0.15 and at least two family contrasts have opposite signs with intervals excluding zero.

B-NOTATION: the D_N interval lies wholly at or below negative 0.10, core manipulation checks pass, and no higher harm predicate fires.

B-SCAFFOLD: D_S lies wholly at or below negative 0.10, D_N lies wholly inside negative 0.05 to positive 0.05, manipulation checks pass, and no harm fires.

B-NULL: D_N, D_S, and D_P intervals each lie wholly inside negative 0.05 to positive 0.05, census is adequate, manipulation checks pass, and no harm fires.

B-INCONCLUSIVE: the fallback for inadequate precision, failed core manipulation, inadequate census, unmatched substantive predicate, or limiting lineage/scoring evidence. It is not null.

All receipts are pilot-scale. The banked label does not erase other predicate statuses, secondary opportunity-weighted estimates, family/subject findings, utility, refusal, completeness, truncation, SHAM status, shared roots, or unknowns. SHAM never selects or rescues an efficacy branch.
