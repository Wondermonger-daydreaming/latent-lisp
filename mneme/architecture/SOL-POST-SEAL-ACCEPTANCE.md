**Standing:** commentary and Architecture 0.1 implementation intent. It does not amend, supersede, or reopen the sealed decisions. Any genuine objection would be named separately as an amendment candidate.

The decisions record is received as controlling.

* state is separated from causal diagnosis;

## L15 — Self-testimony

Accepted without amendment.

A process's unsupported account of its own history has origin `:asserted`, never `:observed`. Architecture 0.1 will preserve the related witness-separation principle: observational standing comes from an inspectable witnessing mechanism at the relevant event boundary, not from the mere fact that testimony was written to disk.

The canonical journal is the default witness for kernel-mediated transitions. It is not made truthful by being called a journal; its standing derives from who writes it, when, under what transition protocol, and with what integrity guarantees.

## L16 — Knowledge exposure and spent blindness

Accepted as kernel-level law.

This is not scoring-specific ornament. Opening a secret changes the future admissibility of principals across many domains: blinded evaluation, sealed prompts, private fixtures, decryption, embargoed material, credentials, and privileged evidence.

Architecture 0.1 will therefore represent epistemic exposure relationally. A secret-opening record must identify at least:

* the protected object;
* the exposing action;
* the principals who received or could inspect it;
* the exposure scope;
* the evidence for that exposure;
* the resulting restrictions on later roles.

The invoker may be among the exposed principals. Self-invocation and kin-invocation will not be treated as exceptional cases. Blindness is not a decorative boolean; it is a resource that can be retained, narrowed, or spent.

## L17 — Ergonomics as conformance

Accepted without weakening.

The phrase "the lawful route may not be longer than the bypass" will remain constitutional language. Architecture 0.1 will supplement it with testable conformance obligations rather than dissolve it into a vague usability aspiration.

The lawful public interface for a supported consequential operation must automatically carry the required attempt identity, authority check, effect record, journaling, and structured outcome. A convenience accessor may not silently discard those semantics.

Any raw host escape that bypasses them must be explicit, visibly unsafe, and outside the ordinary conforming operation surface. The kernel must not build a one-line counterfeit beside a ten-line lawful form and then express surprise when the counterfeit flourishes at dawn.
