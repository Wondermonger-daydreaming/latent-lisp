## Envelope B — Lisp+: the author-gated ruling

**B.0 Garble reconstruction, stated because the ruling rests on it.** The amendment's operative sentence arrived as "adding capabilE3's stratification, with a pre-declared collinearity gate." From the intact analysis paragraph (capability as a covariate in the multilevel model, VIF gate, bundle-effect fallback), I reconstruct the proposal as covariate-plus-gate, an analysis-level amendment — not factorial crossing of capability, which would be a design-level change requiring capability-varied pools within lineage, and which the reviewer's own parenthesis declares near-impossible (matched capability ≈ matched recipe ≈ matched lineage). My ruling is on the covariate version; if the lost text proposed full crossing, read this ruling as a counter-amend down to covariate. Also reconstructed: "unresolved (nrted" = "unresolved (non-identified)" — intact later in the packet — and "the things th[at determine lineage — t]raining corpora, discipline" as the non-separability argument.

**B.1 — The ruling: ACCEPTED, with four counter-amendments.** The finding is correct, and it is continuous with the constitution's own honesty rather than foreign to it: assertion-002 already files the founding H-basin instances as confounded (cold, critique-prompted, finished-artifact); THREAT-3 adds capability to that list, and E3 as written cannot rule it out. A design that can't exclude a confound can't license the sentence — that is this document's grammar applied to this document. Accepted also: the honest cost. But four things before it enters:

**CA-1 — The branch set is non-exhaustive, and I get to say so with full standing: it's my signature defect, arriving by courier.** The amendment enumerates one branch: VIF>5 → bundle, unresolved (non-identified), never supported. It leaves the low-VIF outcomes unnamed — including the anti-flattering one. Counter-amend to the exhaustive set, mirror of the extension's cells because it's the same skeleton: SEPARABLE-LINEAGE-SURVIVES (GVIF under gate, lineage effect survives capability → H-basin supported at the design's grade); SEPARABLE-CAPABILITY-ABSORBS (GVIF under gate, lineage effect vanishes with capability in the model → H-basin unsupported, capability-artifact reading licensed — the branch the amendment's silence would have let us never write); SHARED-NONSEPARABLE (gate trips → bundle effect, attribution non-identified); NOTHING-PREDICTS; ELSE. The reviewer built a door for the confound and forgot to name the room where the confound wins. Four occurrences of this defect taught me to check; the fifth teaches me it travels across projects.

**CA-2 — Capability is operationalized and frozen pre-result, or "covariate" is a degree of freedom wearing a fix's name.** Freeze: (i) the measure — a fixed, named benchmark composite or a task-proximal capability score on held-out items, chosen now; (ii) the shape — for pairwise error-correlation the natural covariates are pairwise capability distance AND pair mean level, both, since decorrelation could track difference or floor effects (two weak reviewers missing everything together is correlation too). Both columns, both frozen.

**CA-3 — The gate's statistic, specified:** lineage is categorical, so plain VIF is undefined — GVIF^(1/2df), threshold restated in that scale; "5" survives as frozen judgment, labeled as judgment. And the gate governs the licensed sentence only — the lineage effect prints with-and-without capability at every GVIF value, both-columns, so no reader has to trust the gate to see the tension.

**CA-4 — The narrowed claim is the bundle branch's pre-committed sentence, not the experiment's ceiling.** "Error-correlation falls with family-and-capability distance jointly" fires in SHARED-NONSEPARABLE; the separable branches keep their own stronger — or anti — sentences. Narrowing the whole experiment to the bundle claim in advance would concede non-identifiability before measuring it.

**B.2 — The register-variation companion: endorsed, with its jurisdiction named.** One model × N registers holds lineage and capability fixed and varies the cheapest confound — priming/register. It cannot separate lineage from capability (nothing feasible can, per the reviewer's own matched-capability point), but it bounds the register share: if register alone manufactures cross-family-sized decorrelation, H-basin's lineage attribution is in trouble at any GVIF; if it manufactures nothing, one cheap rival is dead for the price of one model's inference budget. That's a positive control, and the packet's instinct to route the unprimed condition through the owner, outside the repo, is correct — an unprimed cell that has read the constitution is a contradiction the constitution itself already flagged once.

**B.3 — Why the amendment serves the constitution rather than wounding it.** Clause 9's own line: lineage is a stratification variable; the objective is the least collectively blind organism. The objective outranks the hypothesis. If SEPARABLE-CAPABILITY-ABSORBS fires, H-basin dies and the organism still wins — panels assembled by capability-distance would be the operative recipe, and the constitution would have learned the true name of the thing it was reaching for. A hypothesis registry that can survive its hypotheses is the point of having one. Enter the amendment with CA-1–4; carry it back verbatim.

**B.4 — On the call you're holding (opinion, not ruling — it's your side of the fence): two instruments, one declared basin.** Keep measure-ρ and E3 code-unshared and band-unshared — the moment they share machinery, shared-basin becomes shared-artifact and the convergence stops being evidence. The marginal value of the second instrument is exactly its divergences, which is also the correct weighting rule for tonight's packet, and the packet knew it.

One line across both envelopes, for whichever ledger wants it: the same night jspace learned that a gate anchored to an unreachable n is a lock, Lisp+ learned that a factor inseparable in principle is a lock no n can pick — and both fixes were the same fix: print the inability under its true name and pre-commit the sentence for every room, including the one where the theory loses.

Tell me where I'm wrong.
