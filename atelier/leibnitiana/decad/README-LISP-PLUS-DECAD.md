# The Lisp+ Atelier Decad

Ten executable chambers developed from the conversation around homoiconicity,
resource dependence, bounded transformation, synthesis, survival, custody,
silence, incantation, resonance, dilation, and poetic concord.

They are not proposed as Book 0 constitutional law.  They belong to the atelier:
finite instruments and adversarial specimens that pressure-test distinctions
which may later deserve more neutral formulation elsewhere.

## The procession

1. **`de-foeno.lisp` — Concerning Hay**  
   A representation of nourishment is not nourishment.  Interpreters consume
   substrate even while manipulating descriptions of substrate.

2. **`de-torno.lisp` — Concerning the Lathe**  
   A transformer proposes edits; the engine checks jurisdiction, preconditions,
   budget, and retained shavings before explicit commitment.

3. **`de-fornace.lisp` — Concerning the Furnace**  
   Convergence is not corroboration; operative selection is not epistemic
   settlement; rejected alternatives remain slag.

4. **`de-temperie.lisp` — Concerning Tempering**  
   Repaired survival is not unaided survival.  Rejected futures and repair scars
   remain in the receipt.

5. **`de-leviathan.lisp` — Concerning Leviathan**  
   A hook gives a handle, not the whole creature.  Counterfeit, theft,
   unauthorized transfer, partition, probing, and ownership remain distinct.

6. **`de-abysso.lisp` — Concerning the Deep**  
   Answer, bounded absence, refusal, timeout, occlusion, and in-transit response
   must not collapse into bare `NIL`.

7. **`de-incantatione.lisp` — Concerning Incantation**  
   Internal echo is not discharge; formal closure is not authority; a symbolic
   chamber-effect is not metaphysical proof.

8. **`de-resonantia.lisp` — Concerning Resonance**  
   Resemblance, transmission, entrainment, inheritance, descent, and identity
   are separately receipted relations.

9. **`de-dilatatione.lisp` — Concerning Dilation**  
   Preservation is not petrification; ascent is not subtraction; capacity is not
   communion; an open successor rule is not a completed infinity.

10. **`de-concordia.lisp` — Concerning Concord**  
    Sensual activation, sympathy, movement, and poetic belief arrive in order.
    Their accumulation becomes a world only through explicit mutual support,
    while poetic belief remains `:ASSERTED`, never self-promoted to evidence.

## Cross-chamber vocabulary

The decad keeps several recurring distinctions alive:

```text
representation    != substrate
proposal          != commitment
convergence       != corroboration
repair            != unaided survival
handle            != whole
silence           != absence
closure           != authority
resonance         != identity
growth            != replacement
aggregation       != concord
```

Every chamber keeps a bounded claim, exact nonclaims, named failures, retained
residue, synthetic resource accounting where relevant, and a receipt that may
be recomputed but cannot lawfully promote its own standing.

## Receiving posture

Inspect the live repository before landing anything.  These files were prepared
against the public 2026-07-12 structure, but the branch may have evolved.  Run
sender-side Python checks, then native `sbcl --script` from each specimen's own
directory, then the full atelier suite.  Integrate only those chambers that earn
native receipts.

The decad's refrain:

> The spell needs an interpreter.  
> The interpreter needs hay.  
> The lathe needs jurisdiction.  
> The furnace needs a warrant.  
> The tempered artifact remembers every scar.  
> Leviathan remains more than every handle.  
> The abyss must not be called empty because nothing surfaced.  
> The rhyme closes the circuit without annexing the world.  
> The forms answer one another; the receipt keeps their names.  
> The heart dilates without making eternity and creation competitors.  
> The world coheres because its differences support one another.
