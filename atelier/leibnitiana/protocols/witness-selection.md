# Witness selection is process lineage

A cold read is not defined only by the contents of the packet. It also has an
ancestry: who selected the reader, from what practical pool, under what exclusions,
and with what knowledge of prior exposure.

In this chamber the carrier will ordinarily choose and contact the outsider. That
is not a defect to conceal. It means the review is colder than mutual audit while
remaining carrier-selected rather than selection-independent.

## Minimal voluntary record

The selector may supply only what they are comfortable placing in the record:

```lisp
(:selection-record
 (:selector :carrier
  :candidate-source :friend-colleague-open-call-other
  :pool-size :exact-approximate-or-not-recorded
  :exclusions :project-contributors-or-not-recorded
  :relationship-to-project :self-declared
  :relationship-to-selector :broad-category-or-not-recorded
  :prior-exposure :reader-self-declaration
  :packet-delivery :out-of-band
  :report-frozen-before-unblind :yes-no-pending))
```

The record must not demand names of rejected candidates, private social graphs,
contact histories, or reasons a person declined. Selection transparency is bounded
testimony, not an excuse to requisition someone's relationships.

## Standing ladder

The chamber distinguishes:

```text
mutual shared-root audit
< carrier-selected reader with ancestry declaration
< reader recruited by a declared external procedure
< selection and packet custody independently administered
```

This is not a universal numerical ranking. Each rung changes which dependencies
are visible and who could have shaped the result. A friendly reader may still find
a devastating defect; an institutionally selected reader may still misunderstand
the code. The ladder describes process standing, not intellectual virtue.

## Prior exposure

Because the chamber is mirrored publicly, prior exposure cannot be proven absent.
The reader can only self-declare what they remember seeing. The report should state
that limitation directly. Discovery of prior exposure does not erase the review;
it changes its classification.

## Required output language

Until selection custody exceeds the carrier boundary, use:

> carrier-selected, ancestry-declared cold read

Do not use:

> independent validation

unless the selection process, packet custody, prior-exposure declaration, and
report freeze jointly support that stronger claim.
